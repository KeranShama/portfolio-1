SAFE Check: Aligned with personal interest in GenAI (evidenced by NeuroGen session project). Future-facing and relevant to industry direction — risk managed by building strong traditional QA foundations first.

Senior QA / QA Lead Role in 3 Years

Progress to a Senior QA Engineer or QA Lead position, responsible for defining test strategy, mentoring junior testers, and ensuring quality standards across product releases.
